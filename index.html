ASAP-SQL: Talking to the Data via Active Schema-linking and Agentic Profiling
Bowen Cao, Weibin Liao, Yushi Sun+, Dong Fang+, Haitao Li, Wai Lam

KDD under review, 2026, + indicates correspondence.

We propose ASAP-SQL, an Agentic Text-to-SQL Framework that shifts the paradigm from passive translation to active interrogation. Our framework employs a hypothesis-verification loop to ground model reasoning in empirical data evidence. In the schema linking phase, we combine logical planning with dual-pathway pruning to efficiently reduce the search space, followed by parallel agentic exploration to validate the functional roles of candidate tables and a global synthesis step to ensure topological connectivity. For SQL generation, we introduce a deterministic retrieval mechanism to provide query-dependent guidance, enabling dynamic agentic exploration to resolve value ambiguities and validate implementation logic.

MedKGI: Iterative Differential Diagnosis with Medical Knowledge Graphs and Information-Guided Inquiring
Qipeng Wang, Rui Sheng, Yafei Li, Huamin Qu, Yushi Sun+, Min Zhu+

Submitted to ACL, 2026, + indicates correspondance.

We proposed MedKGI, a diagnostic framework grounded in clinical practices. MedKGI integrates a medical knowledge graph (KG) to constrain reasoning to validated medical ontologies, selects questions based on information gain to maximize diagnostic efficiency, and adopts an OSCE-format structured state to maintain consistent evidence tracking across turns.
